# CellRX Website Design Ideas

## Response 1 — Dark Luxury Medical
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury / High-End Medical Concierge  
**Core Principles:** Authority through restraint; dark navy backgrounds with gold accents; editorial-grade typography hierarchy; clinical precision meets bespoke luxury  
**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#051229) as the dominant ground — communicates trust, depth, and exclusivity. CellRX blue (#0047BB) as the primary action color. Warm gold (#C9A84C) as the accent for premium sections. Cream (#F6F5EC) for text on dark backgrounds.  
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial layout — full-bleed hero with diagonal photo crop, offset section headers, alternating left/right content panels. Avoids centered grid monotony.  
**Signature Elements:** Thin gold horizontal rules as section dividers; large Bebas Neue display numerals as decorative elements (e.g., "01", "02" for service steps); subtle grain texture overlay on dark sections  
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins; hover states that reveal gold underlines on nav; service cards that lift with a subtle shadow on hover  
**Animation:** Staggered entrance animations for hero text; parallax on hero image; counter animations for statistics (e.g., "500+ patients")  
**Typography System:** Bebas Neue for all display headlines; Tenor Sans for subheadings; Inter for body copy. Hierarchy: 72px display → 36px section heads → 18px body
</text>

## Response 2 — Clinical Minimalism
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss Clinical Minimalism  
**Core Principles:** Maximum white space; monochromatic with single blue accent; data-forward trust signals; structured grid with deliberate asymmetry  
**Color Philosophy:** Near-white (#FAFAFA) background with navy text. CellRX blue (#0047BB) used sparingly for CTAs only. No gradients, no textures.  
**Layout Paradigm:** 12-column grid with strict alignment; large margins; content never wider than 960px  
**Signature Elements:** Thin blue vertical lines as section markers; circular physician portrait frames; numbered process steps  
**Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal animation; content speaks for itself; hover states are subtle color shifts  
**Animation:** Fade-in only; no parallax; no scroll triggers  
**Typography System:** Libre Franklin for all text; weight variation (300/600/800) creates hierarchy
</text>

## Response 3 — Editorial Dark Luxury (SELECTED)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Editorial Dark Luxury — inspired by high-end men's health publications and private members' clubs  
**Core Principles:** Deep authority through dark backgrounds; editorial asymmetry; photography-forward; trust through specificity (numbers, names, credentials)  
**Color Philosophy:** Navy-black (#051229) as primary background; CellRX blue (#0047BB) for interactive elements; warm cream (#F6F5EC) for body text on dark; pure white for headlines; gold (#C9A84C) for premium accents and dividers  
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed sections alternating between dark navy and cream; hero uses diagonal geometric crop; physician section uses magazine-style portrait + editorial text layout; service cards use horizontal scroll on mobile, 3-column grid on desktop  
**Signature Elements:** (1) Thin gold horizontal rules between sections; (2) Large Bebas Neue display numerals as decorative anchors; (3) Frosted glass cards for testimonials  
**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll-triggered reveals; magnetic hover on CTAs; smooth page transitions between routes  
**Animation:** Hero text staggers in from left; statistics count up on viewport entry; service cards animate in with a slight upward drift; parallax on full-bleed images  
**Typography System:** Bebas Neue (display, 64-96px) + Tenor Sans (subheadings, 20-28px) + Inter (body, 16-18px). All caps for section labels. Letter-spacing: 0.15em on labels.
</text>

</response>
